Battle with Addiction
Unfortunately, Mac Miller struggled with addiction for many years and it was the cause of his untimely death. The world was devastated when the news about another young artist overdosing. Mac started smoking weed at a young age but unfortunately it evolved into other substances like cocaine. His death was due to mixed drug toxicity of fentanyl, cocaine, and alcohol. Addiction is such a terrible, prominent thing. In the year 2020 alone, there were over 81,000 deaths related to overdoses in the United States alone.
